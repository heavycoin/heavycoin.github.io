Launch schedule

Heavycoin has the potential to achieve massive adoption and become a wide-reaching crypto-currency. With a strong community behind it, Heavycoin could even challenge Litecoin for position number two next to Bitcoin. With this in mind, the Heavycoin launch is taking place in multiple stages to encourage a diverse body of early adopters and stakeholders. Heavycoin will also leverage multiple launch strategies for maximum impact.

Dates and info

  • IPO dates: Feb 17th - March 3rd, 2014
  • Mining starts: March 8th 12:00 UTC (bitcointalk.org forum time)
  • Total supply: 63,000,000 - 128,000,000 HVC (depending on decentralized voting)
  • Premine: 1-2% (depending on total supply) for future development, support and maintenance
  • Wallets: Windows, Ubuntu, Debian and Max OSX available at launch

Each launch stage focuses on a specific user group, such as miners, investors, developers, marketeers and journalists. Our aim is that each group will be given a chance to become an early adopter and bring their unique talents and expertise to strengthen the Heavycoin community. IPO details

To invest in the Heavycoin IPO, simply send Bitcoin to the Heavycoin IPO address using one of the procedures outlined below. Investments must be received before 00:00 UTC Tues 3rd of March, 2014. Funds received after this time will be returned to sender, with the possible loss of the minimum Bitcoin transaction fees.

  • Cut-off date: Mon March 3rd 00:00 UTC
  • Heavycoin IPO address {{ site.HVCIPOAddr }}
  • Total IPO investment received so far: Loading... BTC
  • Live Investment Updates

The 5,000,000 HVC will be distributed among the IPO investors proportional to how much BTC they have invested. There is no limit on the amount of BTC investment. The free market will determine the HVC exchange rate. For example, if there were only two investors, Alice and Bob, and Alice invests 1 BTC and Bob invests 0.25 BTC, then Alice would get 80% (4M HVC), Bob would get 20% (1M HVC) and the HVC exchange rate would be 0.00000025 BTC per HVC.

Heavycoin IPO investors will be paid their HVC stakes instantly the moment the Heavycoin genesis block is released on March 8th, as soon as the network is launched and mining starts. There will be no lengthy waiting period nor will there be a manual, error-prone disbursement of funds. It will be an automatic process leveraging the genesis block. IPO security

  • We will send you a bitcointalk.org PM confirming your IPO payment has been received.
  • A few days before the release on March 8th, we will publish the current list of IPO stakeholders and we will attempt to resolve any issues that may occur.
  • If we encounter any conflicts where multiple bitcointalk.org handles are claiming the same Bitcoin transaction Id, then we will contact the respective users and the legitimate key holder will be able to prove she owns the sending Bitcoin address by sending us a message signed by the corresponding Bitcoin private key(s). Thus, any attempts to cheat will be completely futile.
  • Advanced users who want to to sign their forum posts from the start are encouraged to do so. In this case you must send your IPO payment from a single address and then sign their IPO forum post with the corresponding private key. We can then verify that the Heavycoin Public Key does indeed correspond to that particular BTC payment. Sending payments from a specific address can be achieved from the Bitcoin RPC interface OR using blockchain.info.

Mining details

Heavycoin mining will open up a new dynamic because of decentralized block reward voting. Miners who are greedy and have little stake could vote for the maximum block reward of 1024. However, this will increase the supply of Heavycoin and potentially decrease the price. Other miners, who are not greedy, or who already have a sufficient stake, might vote for a lower block reward -- even zero -- in order to decrease the supply and to slow the minting rate. Either way, this is going to be interesting!

CPU-only: uses HEFTY1, a novel approach to CPU-only proof-of-work
Ultra-secure: SHA-256, Keccak-512, Grøestl-512, BLAKE-512
Block time: 2 Min
Retarget up: Every 5 blocks (max 100%)
Retarget down: Every block (max 200%)
Block reward: 1024 max (decentralized block reward voting)
Total supply: 63M to 128M HVC (depending on votes)
Multipool protection: Kimoto Gravity Well Temporal Retargeting
Premine: 1-2% (depending on total supply) for future development, support and maintenance
